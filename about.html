More About Me

Hometown

I live in the city of Louisville, Kentucky. Louisville is a fairly large city and is home to a large number of pool players. Many local tournaments are hosted here with great payouts and the skill here tends to be higher than other cities.

I had played pool for fun with family and friends, but have never thought that it would be something that I would be passionate about. I didn't start shooting pool until I was about 24. I joined a league with my dad and my uncle, playing at a local bar every Thursday afternoon.

It definitely started off very frustrating, with me losing most of my games, but I quickly became addicted and wanted to improve. I learned from players around me and practiced every chance that I could. Over the following years, I would improve and win more and more of my games.

I have now been playing pool for a little over nine years. I still shoot in my league every thursday afternoon, though my team has changed a few times. I also shoot in several tournaments throughout the year, with my largest payout being a little over a thousand dollars.

If you want to learn more about pool or if you're considering joining a league, you can visit the APA website for more information!

Eric Fentress 2020
