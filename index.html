Introduction to Pool

History of Billiards

Billiards is a game that has been around for hundreds of years. The game has been played by kings and knights and is now a fun recreational sport for people of all ages. It is a casual game played for fun and is even played on a professional level to win thousands of dollars.

All About the Basics

Whether you are just wanting to improve so you can beat your friends or wanting to step up and play competitively, it all starts with the basics. Without the fundamentals, you have no foundation to build on. Visit the Basics page to get started on your journey.

Have Fun

Pool can be frustrating, inconsistent and takes a considerable amount of skill to reach higher levels. You may also notice the amount of luck (or lack there of) involved when playing. No matter the outcome of your game, at the end of the day, have fun. Enjoy the game and always push to be better. Frustration never helps. So, if you find yourself wanting to snap that pool stick over your leg, take a break and remember that it's just a game!
