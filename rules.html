The Rules

8 Ball

This is the most popular game played when someone wants to play a game of pool using all 15 of the numbered balls. Rules are subject to change depending on who you are playing with or what league you are in, but the general rules are all the same.

  1. Rack the balls with the 8 ball in the middle.
  2. One player is stripes and the other is solids, determined by who makes a ball first.
  3. Pocket all of your balls, call your pocket on the 8 ball and make it to win.

9 Ball

Not similar to 8 ball. This game only uses 9 of the balls from 1 to 9.

  1. Rack the balls numbered 1 through 9 in a diamond formation.
  2. The 1 ball must be in the front and the 9 ball in the middle.
  3. Balls must be made in numerical order.
  4. The person who makes the 9 ball wins.

10 Ball

Very similar to 9 ball only you use balls 1 through 10.

  1. Rack the balls numbered 1 through 10 in a triangle formation.
  2. The 1 ball must be in the front and the 10 ball in the middle.
  3. Balls must be made in numerical order.
  4. The person who makes the 10 ball wins.
